Service / Servicio

Throughout my career, I have actively contributed to initiatives that foster collaboration and growth within the data science and artificial intelligence community. From mentorship programs to organizing academic events and engaging in science communication, my goal is to support knowledge development and increase accessibility to technology.

I have worked on projects aimed at closing the gap in access to data science education, supporting new talents in their training, and helping professionals enhance their skills. Additionally, I collaborate with institutions and communities to promote the ethical and responsible use of artificial intelligence.

I firmly believe in the power of knowledge-sharing and collective learning. By mentoring aspiring data scientists, participating in open-source projects, and advocating for inclusivity in tech, I remain committed to fostering a more diverse and prepared community for the challenges of the future.
