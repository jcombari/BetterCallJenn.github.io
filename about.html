About Me

Since I was little, I have been fascinated by the power of knowledge. I was the child who always asked *why*, the teenager who loved solving puzzles, and the adult who found in data science a way to connect curiosity with real-world impact.

Over the years, I have explored the world of artificial intelligence, deep learning, and cloud computing, not only as a scientist but also as a mentor and communicator. I believe that knowledge should be shared, and I love helping others understand and apply AI to solve meaningful challenges.
