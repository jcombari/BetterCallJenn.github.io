Teaching / Docencia

Throughout my career, I have combined my passion for data science with a strong commitment to sharing knowledge. I have guided students and professionals on their journey to mastering data analysis, machine learning, and artificial intelligence, focusing on practical and real-world applications. My teaching approach is centered on making complex topics accessible and understandable, fostering critical thinking and curiosity. From statistical methodologies to the development of advanced deep learning models, my goal is to empower others with the tools they need to innovate and make data-driven decisions. Whether through classes, mentorships, or science communication, I enjoy being part of the growth of those eager to dive into this fascinating field.
